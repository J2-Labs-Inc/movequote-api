Our Commitment: Your business data is sensitive. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information and give you control over your data.

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (HTTPS). This prevents eavesdropping and tampering.

Local-First Storage

Your business data (quotes, clients, settings) is stored locally in your browser by default. This means your data stays on your device and isn't automatically transmitted to our servers.

Secure Authentication

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with unique salts. We never store plain-text passwords. Sessions are managed with secure, HTTP-only cookies.

Infrastructure Security

We use Cloudflare for DDoS protection, WAF (Web Application Firewall), and bot mitigation. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, SOC 2 compliant platforms.

Your Security Responsibilities

Security is a shared responsibility. We recommend:

  • Use a strong, unique password for your MoveQuote account
  • Don't share your credentials with others
  • Log out when using shared or public computers
  • Keep your browser updated to receive security patches
  • Export backups of your data regularly using the built-in export feature

Data Backup & Export

You can export all your data at any time:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management
  • Click "Export All Data"
  • Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file

We recommend keeping regular backups, especially before clearing browser data.

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting your data:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours
  • We will provide clear information about what happened and what data was affected
  • We will take immediate steps to contain and remediate the issue
  • We will cooperate with relevant authorities as required by law

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

  • Email us at security@getgetmovequote.com
  • Provide detailed information about the vulnerability
  • Do not publicly disclose the issue until we've had a chance to address it

We appreciate security researchers who help us keep MoveQuote safe.
